Swami Vivekananda Kendriya Vidyalaya

It was inaugurated on 3rd January, 2013. The school is a co-educational English medium institution. Adding one class a year, the school has reached standard X in 2017 and received affiliation from CBSE, New Delhi in 2017 vide Affiliation number - 230162, Code No. 35248

Organisation and Management:

The School is run by Swami Vivekananda Trust which is a non profitable charitable trust registered with Regd No 429/2006, under a Deed of Trust as per Indian Trust Act 1882.

The registered trust established with the noble mission of building a strong society through the ideals of Indian Hindu monk Swami Vivekananda. It also aims to promote quality educational services, Skill development and vocational courses for the people living in rural and tribal areas at an affordtable cost.
